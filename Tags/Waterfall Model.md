### Overview:

- A **linear, step-by-step model**.
    
- Each stage (planning, design, coding, testing, etc.) must be **completed before the next starts**.
    
- Includes **verification and validation** at each stage.
    

### ✅ Best For:

- **Well-understood problems** with **fixed requirements**.
    
- Projects where **early planning and documentation** are critical.
    

### ❌ Limitations:

- **Inflexible**—difficult to go back and revise earlier stages.
    
- Late discovery of errors can be **very costly**.
